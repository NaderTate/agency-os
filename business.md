## Mission

We build real, code-backed AI systems for local service businesses so they stop losing revenue to missed calls, slow lead response, and manual scheduling. The product side handles the customer; the agency side handles the owner.

## Why we exist

The AI-receptionist and voice-agent market is dominated by no-code overlays that fake the demo and break in production. Owner-operated businesses deserve infrastructure that actually works, with code we own, deployed in their stack, on a recurring relationship.

## ICP (who we serve)

Owner-operated local service businesses with phones as a core revenue channel. Sweet spot:

- **Verticals:** dental, med spa, law (PI / family), home services, auto repair, chiropractic, salons / aesthetics.
- **Size:** 1-3 locations, $500k-$5M annual revenue.
- **Pain felt:** owner is in the chair / on the road and the phone goes unanswered; cancellations leave empty slots; new leads pick whoever calls back first.
- **Buying signal:** they say "we keep missing calls" or "we tried [no-code thing] and it broke."

## Who we don't serve

- Enterprises with ERP / legacy systems and procurement cycles.
- Other agencies (we sell to operators, not resellers).
- Pure B2C apps.
- Anything where voice isn't the channel.
