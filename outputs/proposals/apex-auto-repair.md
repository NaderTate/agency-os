# Proposal: Voice Agent for Apex Auto Repair

**Prepared by Aether AI — 2026-05-07**

## The problem

The phone is a tax on every billable hour at the shop. Techs are under cars, you're answering between jobs, and half the calls get missed — booking calls and a constant stream of "is my car ready yet?" status checks that interrupt the work without adding a dime. Missed booking calls are lost jobs; the status calls just eat the day.

## What we'll build

A voice agent that takes the phone off your team's hands:

- **Answers every call** so booking calls stop going missed.
- **Books appointments** against your shop schedule.
- **Handles status calls** — "is my car ready?" — by checking the job status and answering, no human needed.
- **Texts customers automatically** when their car is ready for pickup.
- **Routes anything it can't handle** to you with context, so you only pick up when it matters.

## Scope

**Included:** inbound call answering, appointment booking, automated status responses, ready-for-pickup texts, and escalation routing. Configured to your services and schedule.

**Out of scope (for now):** parts ordering and invoicing integration. Addable later.

## Timeline

About 2–3 weeks: discovery + setup (week 1), build + your review (week 2), go-live and tuning (week 3).

## Investment

- **Setup: $5,000** (one-time)
- **Monthly: $1,000/mo** (hosting, voice + AI usage, outbound texts, monitoring, and support)

*Example math (your numbers will differ):* if catching missed booking calls adds even two extra jobs a week, and the team stops losing time to status calls, the system pays for itself many times over each month.
